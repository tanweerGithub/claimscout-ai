PATENT SPECIFICATION
Patent Number: US-1102938-A1
Title: Precision Volumetric Sauce Dispensation Grid
Owner: FoodTech Automation Labs
Year: 2022

SUMMARY:
A patent describing a fixed grid of overhead condiment dispenser valves. Pressurized air pistons compress volumetric chambers to dispense precise portions of ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise onto buns arranged in a matching layout tray.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION & CLAIMS:
What is claimed is:
1. An apparatus for high-speed food assembly, comprising:
   - A grid of at least six overhead dispenser valves containing volumetric chambers for viscous liquids;
   - A pneumatic air system configured to compress the volumetric chambers simultaneously;
   - Wherein the grid dispensates precise condiment portions onto a tray of buns placed directly underneath the valves.
