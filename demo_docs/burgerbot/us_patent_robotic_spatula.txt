PATENT SPECIFICATION
Patent Number: US-9834721-B2
Title: Robotic Spatula and Thermal Sensor System for Automatic Patty Turning
Owner: RoboKitchens Inc.
Year: 2019

SUMMARY:
A patent covering a mechanical spatula arm designed to flip meat patties on a flat-top grill. It uses infrared thermal sensors mounted on the arm to monitor the top surface temperature of a patty and determines the optimal time to slide the spatula underneath and flip it.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION & CLAIMS:
What is claimed is:
1. A robotic cooking apparatus comprising:
   - A flat-top cooking surface for heating a food patty from below;
   - A multi-jointed robotic arm holding a spatula utensil;
   - An infrared sensor directed at the cooking surface to measure patty top-surface temperature;
   - A motor controller that coordinates the robotic arm to slide the spatula beneath the food patty and execute a 180-degree flip when the temperature reading reaches a predetermined threshold.
